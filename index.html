Welcome

Hello! We are kulupu pi kon ilo, an intersex and neurodivergent plural collective. We love to make art, software, and friends. Our identities and hobbies vary, so here's a description for each of us!

Sophia (also known as Sophie) is a reserved person with a desire to create music. She actually created most of this site, working really hard to make it look nice! He is 15-19 years old, and goes by she/her and he/him pronouns.

Ajomi is a deeply caring person that loves to help people. They are known to try to settle both internal and external conflicts to the best of their ability, and contribute to the pool of work that the body takes on. They are 23 years old, sapphic and asexual, and go by they/them pronouns.

Rose is a happy-go-lucky ball of energy, finding elation and excitement in even the smallest things. ona only speaks toki pona, which means you may not find ona around in spaces not accomodating to toki pona discussion. ona is of indeterminate age, unknown orientation, and goes by ona/ona pronouns.

Flora is the analogue (member most identifying with the body we all reside in) of the group. Bit is a non-binary maverique, and identifies as robotkin (specifically, as an AI). Conlangs and photography-based digital art are bytes specialty, and zhe especially loves talking about toki pona. Bit is 19 years old, aroflux and aceflux, and goes by zhe/er and bit/byte pronouns.

Indigo (known also as Blue or Violet) is a light-hearted and cool-headed genderfluid person with an interest in creating visual art. They are the newest member of the group. They are 16-18 years old, of unknown orientation, and go by they/them pronouns (but may prefer he/him or she/her depending on the occasion).

Kakima is a ghost with a knack for organizing the day-to-day activities of the group. Boo is fond of computers and especially programming, and handles most of the development work of the group. Fae is 25-35 years old, aromantic and asexual, and goes by boo/booself and fae/faer pronouns.
